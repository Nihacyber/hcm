## ‚ùì FAQ

### Q: Where is the teacher login page?
A: At `/teacher-login` route (http://localhost:3002/#/teacher-login)

### Q: How do I view a teacher's profile?
A: Click on the teacher's name in the Teachers table

### Q: How do I copy credentials?
A: Click the [Copy] button next to username or password

### Q: Can I edit teacher information from the profile?
A: Not from the profile modal. Use the Edit button in the table.

### Q: Does it work on mobile?
A: Yes, fully responsive design

### Q: Does it support dark mode?
A: Yes, full dark mode support

### Q: What if copy doesn't work?
A: Check browser permissions, try different browser, or manually copy

### Q: Can teachers see their own profile?
A: Teachers can see their dashboard at `/teacher-dashboard`

### Q: How are credentials generated?
A: Username is firstname.lastname, password is 10 random characters

### Q: Where are credentials stored?
A: In Firebase Firestore database
